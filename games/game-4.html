Imposter

You and three bots play Imposter: three players know one secret word; one Imposter does not. Two full rounds — each player says exactly one English word per turn (letters only, not the secret word, not containing it). Then everyone votes once for who is the Imposter. Majority eliminates one player; if that player was the Imposter, the Innocents win.

    Vote for the Imposter

    You cannot vote for yourself. Pick one bot.

    Bots use Gemini with the same clue and vote rules as a human. Add GEMINI_API_KEY on Vercel (or .env.local with vercel dev).
