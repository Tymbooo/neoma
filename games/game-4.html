Imposter

You and three bots play Imposter: three players know one secret word; one Imposter does not. Each player says exactly one English word per turn (letters only, not the secret word, not containing it). After round 1 (four clues), you may vote immediately or play round 2 for four more clues—then vote. Everyone votes once for the Imposter; majority eliminates one player. If an innocent is eliminated, the Imposter wins. If the Imposter is eliminated, they get one guess at the word—correct guess steals the win.
